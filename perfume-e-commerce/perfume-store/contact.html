How long does shipping take?

‚ñº
We offer a 30-day return policy on all unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Opened products can be returned within 14 days with a valid reason. Please ensure items are in their original packaging.

Yes! We offer complimentary fragrance consultations both in-store and virtually. Our expert perfumers will help you discover your perfect scent based on your preferences and personality. Book an appointment through our contact form or call us directly.
