Our Journey

Founded in 1995 by master perfumer Marie Dubois, LUXE began as a small atelier in the heart of Paris. With a passion for capturing memories in scent, Marie set out to create fragrances that transcended the ordinary.

What started as a collection of just five signature scents has blossomed into a global luxury brand, renowned for its commitment to quality, artistry, and innovation. Each fragrance is a testament to our dedication to the craft of perfumery.

Today, LUXE continues to push boundaries, blending traditional techniques with modern innovation to create scents that are timeless yet contemporary, classic yet daring.
